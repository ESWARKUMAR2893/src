Frequently Asked Questions

  • MyExpertMedics provides online second medical opinion from highly qualified doctors, leaders in their respective fields. Contact MyExpertMedics when there is uncertainty about a diagnosis or want to reaffirm a medical plan of action.
  • MyExpertMedics team will review the reports provided by you and might advise further specific investigations to confirm a diagnosis. In case there is difference of opinion on a medical plan of management our expert team will help you in explaining the pros and cons of each plan of action.
The physicians we work with for second opinions are experts in their respective fields and include physicians practicing at leading medical hospitals.
Clients can follow the below simple steps:
  1. Start as a new Client by registering the credentials at the Client log in page
  2. You will only need to enter a few basic details to be able to register
  3. Once you are registered you can enter your case details and upload additional supporting client information (for example X-rays, CT Scan, MRI Scan, Blood Reports) into our user friendly website
  4. Upon making the payment Client will get an acknowledgment email from MyExpertMedics team and a timeframe to provide the MyExpertMedics Physician report.
